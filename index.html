How ReUnite Works

Three simple steps to get your belongings back — or help someone else recover theirs.

STEP ONE
📝

Report the Item

Submit a detailed report with item name, category, last known location, and your contact details. Takes under 2 minutes.

STEP TWO
🔍

Admin Reviews & Lists

Our campus admin team verifies the submission and lists it on the public gallery so it's visible to everyone on campus.

STEP THREE
🤝

If you spot your item, use the Enquire button. Admin verifies ownership and coordinates the safe handover. Done!

About Us

Built to Solve a Real Campus Problem

Every semester, hundreds of items are lost across campus — left behind in canteens, libraries, labs, and hostels. Traditional notice boards and word-of-mouth aren't enough.

ReUnite replaces that chaos with a structured, digital platform where lost items are catalogued, verified, and reunited with their owners — all under transparent admin oversight.

Developed by MCA students at School of Management Sciences, this is a real project solving a real problem for thousands of campus members.

FAQ

Common Questions

How do I claim a found item?
Click the "Claim / Enquire" button on any gallery card. An enquiry form will open — fill in your details and the admin team will contact you to verify ownership before handing over the item.
How long does the process take?
Most items are listed within a few hours of being reported. Once you submit a claim, our admin team typically responds within 24 hours and coordinates the handover.
What if my item isn't listed yet?
Submit a Lost Report using the form above. Your item will be listed in the gallery after admin review. You can also submit an Enquiry to let the team know you're actively looking.
How is ownership verified?
Our admin team will ask you to describe identifying features of the item — color, brand, specific marks or stickers. This ensures items are returned only to their rightful owners.
Can I report a found item too?
Absolutely! Use the Report form and describe what you found and where. You can hand the item to campus security or the admin office for safekeeping while we find the owner.
Is my personal data safe?
Yes. Your contact details are only used by our admin team to coordinate item recovery. They are not displayed publicly and are never shared with third parties.
